FAQs

Do you have a question to ask? Find our most asked queries here...

  • Why do parcels need to be left open and inspected by Mediship team?

    We as responsible carrier must make sure that the parcel contain no dangerous or prohibited items and that the description of contents on the parcel label is accurate. Mediship may, at its sole discretion, or upon the request of the competent authorities, open and inspect any medicine shipment without notice at any time, and shall incur no liability of any kind

  • When will my shipment be delivered?

    he delivery time of your shipment depends on the service level you have chosen. Please keep in mind that usual delivery times can vary depending on product type and origin/destination-relation, from 4-5 days for neighbouring countries up to 15 days for countries far away

  • Who can I contact if I have not received my shipment?

    If you have not received your shipment. If they are unable to provide an updated status of your shipment, please contact our local customer service at support@mediship.in

  • Can I change the delivery day or delivery time?

    It is not possible to change the delivery day or delivery time once the shipment has been dispatched.

  • When will my tracking information appear?

    Can I change the delivery day or delivery time?

  • Why is my shipment status unchanged?

    You should see tracking events within 24hours after you have received the tracking number/ID. The reason for this time lag is that in most cases the first tracking event only shows up once the shipment has been handed over to our delivery partners.

  • Why is the minimum weight 500gm / 0.5 Kg ? My medicine is less than that?

    0.5kg (or 500g) is the minimum weight required to get a price quote. We don't have a cheaper price for items weighing less than 0.5kg, so please be assured that you are getting an accurate quote.

  • Can I estimate the weight or dimensions of the package?

    Please do not estimate the weight or dimensions as this may incur extra charges or your parcel being returned. Be as accurate as possible.
     Note: Our delivery partners will re weigh and measure parcels at their service centers before they are dispatched.

  • What are the payment options for Mediship?

    There are several options available to customers; we accept all major debit and credit cards, as well as NEFT.

  • Are there any medicines which I can’t through Mediship?

    Yes, Any medicine which is prohibited by law of the land ( both origin & destination) will not be accepted by Mediship. The Sender must comply with all applicable laws, regulations or rules.
    Note: Mediship may be required by law to report improperly declared or undeclared Shipments of dangerous goods / contraband items to the appropriate local competent authority. The Sender may be subject to fines and penalties under applicable law.

  • Are there any medicines which I can’t through Mediship?

    Shipments which cross national borders may have to be cleared through Customs both in origin and the destination country prior to delivery to the Recipient. Where our delivery partners provides customs clearance, Mediship will on request of Customs provide to Customs all data necessary for clearance, as provided by the Shipper.

  • Is duties and taxes applicable for medicines which are for personal usage?

    Customs may levy customs duties and/or import VAT or any relevant duties and taxes in arrears irrespective of the content / declared value of the shipment. It is import country’s customs sole discretion and uncontrollable for us a carrier. You may have to pay the duties and taxes before getting the delivery from our partners.
